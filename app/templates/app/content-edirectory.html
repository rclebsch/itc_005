ITC and Women associations launched the Women EAC Business E-Directory. This tool is an interactive online directory of business Women engaged in cross border trade in EAC.

Cross-border trade carried out by Women in EAC region involved mainly agricultural products and other manufactured goods. Import and Export activities are seasonal and take place in the main EAC trading corridors. Facilitating business information and contacts among Women facilitates trading by reducing the need to move to one region to another to find tradable goods and while increasing intra-regional trade and trust among operators, also impacting positively on the business environment.

The E-Directory gathers and disseminates not only Women business information open to members and non-members but also the most useful contact details and focal point in EAC region.
